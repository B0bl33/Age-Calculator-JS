Fun Facts About Age

Did you know? By age 30, the average person has walked the equivalent of three times around the Earth.

At age 40, most people will have blinked over 415 million times!

By age 50, your heart will have beaten more than 1.5 billion times.

At age 60, the average person will have shed 100 pounds of skin cells.

Did you know? The longest recorded human lifespan is 122 years!
